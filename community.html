Our community

In the municipality of Lipkovo we’ve always known we have something special. Here, in the heart of beautiful Karadaku, we’ve built a fully bilingual community that we believe captures the essence of healthy country living – a community that is safe, cooperative and friendly, a community that is respectful of its heritage while embracing opportunities for progress, a community that is small enough to care, but is also big enough to prosper.
Living in Lipkovo means you can achieve the quality of life you desire, whether you are a student, employed, running a business or carving out a new retirement lifestyle.Our Council and staff work together to provide necessary services like building permits, roads and landfills, but we also offer many recreational and cultural amenities for everyone to enjoy year round, indoors and in the great outdoors. No matter your age, income or interests, there is something for everyone in Lipkovo.
