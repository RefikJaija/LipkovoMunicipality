Geography

The municipality of Lipkovo consists of 23 villages:Alasevce,Belanovce,Vaksince,Vishtica,Glaznja,Goshince
,Dumanovce,Zlokukjane,Source,Lipkovo,Lojane,Lukare ,Matejche,Nikushtak,Opae,Orizari,Otlja
,R'nkovce,Ropalce,Runica,Slupchane,Guard and streams.
The municipality borders Serbia to the north,Čučer-Sandevo Municipality to the west,the City of Skopje to the southwest,Aračinovo Municipality to the south and Kumanovo Municipality to the east.

Demographics

Government and politics

The mayor of the municipality of Lipkovo is Erkan Arifi from the ranks of BDI . He was elected in the 2017 local elections for a four-year term. [2]
The council of the municipality of Lipkovo is composed of 19 members.
According to the last local elections of 2017, the members of the council according to the political affiliation for the mandate 2017-2021 are:BDI: 9 seats ,BESA: 4 seats,AA: 3 seats,DPA: 2 seats,PDP: 1 seat
